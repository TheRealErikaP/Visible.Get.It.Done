About the AI Button
Your app works great as a to-do list right out of the box. The AI is optional.

To activate it you need an API key from Anthropic. They require a one-time $5 minimum — for daily use this lasts months.
1
Go to platform.claude.com and log in
2
Tap Get API Key on the right side of the dashboard
3
Tap + Create Key, name it anything, tap Create
4
Copy immediately — starts with sk-ant- — you only see it once
5
Open your app, tap ✦, paste your key and tap Save
Not ready? Skip the AI and use the full app. Add it anytime later.
