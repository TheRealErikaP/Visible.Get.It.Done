About the AI Button
Your app works great right out of the box. The AI button is optional — tap it when you're stuck or overwhelmed. To activate it, Anthropic requires a one-time $5 minimum to get started. For casual daily use that lasts months.
1
Go to platform.claude.com and log in with your Claude account
2
On the dashboard tap Get API Key on the right side of the screen
3
Tap + Create Key, give it any name, tap Create
4
Tap Copy immediately — it starts with sk-ant- and you only see it once!
5
Open your app, tap the ✦ button, paste your key and tap Save. Done forever.
The app works great without AI too. Skip it and add it whenever you're ready.
